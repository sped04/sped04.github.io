About us

Surrey Peer ED is a fun club for young people in care, and care leavers, in and around Surrey, UK. We meet regularly in West Byfleet and discuss topics relevant to the group. SPED has been run by volunteers since 2004.

Contact us

We are always open to new members who live in Surrey, UK, and are currently in care or have recently left care. You must be 14 or over at the time of our next residential in July. You can contact us via Facebook, Twitter or good old fashioned email. If none of those options float your boat then you can also talk to your social worker.
